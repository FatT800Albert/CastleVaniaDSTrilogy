Slide Jump addresses:

02048080 = (left), 02048084 = (right) slide jump speed modifier

02048098 = jump height 

020480A8 = Gravity force. Higher the number, smaller the jump

020480E0 (ldrsh r1, [r4, 4h]) = dmg address

020480EC = DMG type

0204813C = bounceback distance from hitting a wall (left)

02048140 = bounceback distance from hitting a wall (right)
