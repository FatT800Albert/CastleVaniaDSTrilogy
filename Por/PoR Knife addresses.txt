Knife addresses:

02210B04 = Middle knife spawn distance address. Smaller the number, closer to player it spawns

02210B08 = Middle knife spawn height caller. 

02210B1C = Bottom knife spawn distance address. Higher the number, closer to player it spawns

02210B20 = Bottom knife spawn height caller

02210B40 = Top knife spawn distance address. Smaller the number, closer to player it spawns

02210B44 = Top knife spawn height caller

02210B98 = Dagger throwing speed
