Backflip pointers:

02206008 (left) 02206010 (right) = speed modifier 

02205FAC = Force caller. Bigger the number, higher you go

02205FB1 = If changed player is able to make backflips even forward

02205FB4 = Invincibility frames calculator. Vanilla value is 20 (14 in hex)

02205FC0 = Gravity caller. Higher the number, lower the jump

02205FC8 = Change value -> space jump

02205FFC = Makes backflips height momentum based. Higher you jump, higher backflip
