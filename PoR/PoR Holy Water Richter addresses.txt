Holy water (Richter) addresses:

02211D50 = Holy water projectiles throw delay

02211D84 = Height caller for projectiles start

02211DC0 = Holy Water's throw distance

02211DC4 = Holy Water throw height index. Higher the number, lower the registered throw height. (prevents Holy Water from clipping and disappearing if thrown under a roof/jump through floor)

02211DD8 = Holy Water's duration counter

02211E68 = Iframes

02211E89 = Holy Water's throw gravity force. Higher the number, floatier throw

02211EFC = Holy Water flames travel speed

02211EFD = Holy Water flames travel speed. Can be used to fine tune it (0E for stationary flame pillar)
